BluBracket Documentation

Track what's in your code, who has access, and where it goes.

What's in your code

Find secrets, PII, non-inclusive language (NIL), and infrastructure as code (IaC) risks before commit, before merge, and before they propagate to others.

Who has access️

Audit who and what systems have access to your code and enable easy collaboration across the organization while protecting against unmanaged access-creep.

Where it goes

Code is a fluid asset, always moving where it needs and finding its own level across the org. BluBracket Enterprise tracks where it goes when it leaks outside the org.
